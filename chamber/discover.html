Cook Inlet

The Cook Inlet, named for Captain Cook, is a wonderful spot for picturesque moments. Whether gazing heavenward to see the Aurora Borealis, or into the water to catch the balugas breaching during migration.

Denali National Park

Nothing is more majestic than Mt. Denali towering over the 6 million acre preserve. Camping, fishing, wildlife and more awaits you in this National Park.

Artic Road Runner

The Artic Road Runner is a burger joint that has been around longer than I have. It is a must have when visiting, burgers, fries, shakes, eaten by the Campbell Creak.

Bike to Thunderbird Falls

This ride is a short one, but it is breathtaking. Hear the roar of the 200 foot waterfall as you wind around the trail. The top is a great picnic spot!

Ice Sculpting Competitions

Hosted outside the Preforming Arts Center in Anchorage, the Ice Sculpting Competitions are sure to please! Fun for all ages and the sculptures are left up for several weeks!

Fur Rondy

Fur Rondezous is Alaska in entirety. Between running with the Caribou, snow sculpting, games and festival food, there is something for everyone!
