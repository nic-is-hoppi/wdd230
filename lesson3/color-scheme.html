Color Scheme

There are two main colors for Denali Chamber's website. the first being a shade of blue hexcoded #adbfd4 and the second, a brown hexcoded #7a3021. The blue will be the background for the header and footer, as well as the hover color for the navigation bar. The brown with be the color for header and footer text and the background color for the navigation.
