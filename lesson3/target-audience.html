Target Audience

This site is for residents and visitors of the Denali area of Alaska. These people are usually outdoorsy, adventurous, and looking for excitement.

Personas

35-55 year old with bachelors degree on vacation to Alaska. Want to find good places to eat and activities to be able to do for cheap.

18-77 year old residents with high school degree, possible higher education. Wants to follow up on news stories and weather in the area.

Scenarios

  • A bear was loose in town and I want to see if it was caught.
  • I've planned a hike in Denali National Park, is it going to rain?
  • What resturaunt has the best burger in the area?
  • I want to see where I can go river rafting.
  • I am looking for information of the wildlife reserve.
